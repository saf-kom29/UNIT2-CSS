NOTE: to qualify for WIC, you must:

  • be pregnant, postpartum (up to six months), breastfeeding (up to one year), or have a chld under five years old.
  • Meet income guidlines (household income at or below 185% of the federal poverty level) or participate in programs like SNAP, Medicaid, or TANF.
  • Be at nutritional risk (determined by a healthcare professional)

    • *If you are a potential applicant, you should bring proof of identity, residency, income, pregnancy document (if there is one), and children's health records to any given appointment.

WIC assists families and mothers by granting money and benefits while shopping in certain districts. If you apply to one of more of these conditions, you can sign up here: WIC Sign Up Page
